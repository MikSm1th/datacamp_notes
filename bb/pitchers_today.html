Definitions

Each player's name is linked to his individual stats page, which includes a table of day-by-day points.

The linked "R" and "W" after a player's name are links to his RotoWorld and RotoWire pages, respectively.

Players who are currently on an active 25-man roster (minors, D/L, etc.) are denoted by an "x" after their games started. Inactive players with zero points YTD are not listed. Inactive status is determined based on current listings at MLB.com, updated hourly.

  • GS stands for "games started"
  • GP stands for "games played".
  • "Last game" points are for the last game played for each player. If the report is for games started only, then this will be the points for the last game started.
  • "Days ago" indicates how many days ago the game was played. ("1" means yesterday.)
  • Column B reflects the point average per GS or GP (depending on the specified choice) during the period selected.

    • A red price indicates a low price for the year, while a green price denotes a high price for the year.

    This report is based upon data which the Guru believes to be reliable, but for which no assurances of accuracy can be made. Users who discover suspected errors are requested to report them to the Guru via email .
